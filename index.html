What is Rick and Morty?

Rick and Morty is a show that follows two characters (you'll never guess their names) throgh a number of weird and whacky adventures. Rick, the grandfather, drags Morty, the grandson, through everything from teleporters to interdimensional portals. These lead them to unkown lands with new creatures and a variety of problems. Why Rick brings Morty with him on these so-called "adventures" is unknown to us as viewers.
